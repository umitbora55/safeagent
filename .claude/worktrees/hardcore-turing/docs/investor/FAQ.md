# Investor FAQ

## Why you?
We focus on provable security and deterministic verification for AI agents. SafeAgent treats security as a release gate, not an afterthought.

## Market size?
Any organization deploying AI automation faces security and compliance blockers. The market includes enterprise AI, compliance-driven teams, and regulated industries.

## How do you compete with agent frameworks?
We differentiate on auditability, local-first security, and verification gates. Most frameworks prioritize autonomy and UX but lack tamper-evident audits.

## Risks?
- Faster-moving competitors with closed platforms.
- Adoption friction for local installs.

Mitigation: clear quickstart, deterministic gates, and enterprise integrations.

## Revenue model?
Open-source core, paid enterprise features: governance, compliance, integrations, support.

## Why now?
AI agents are moving into production. Security and trust are the bottlenecks.
