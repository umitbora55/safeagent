# SafeAgent — One-Slide Pitch

**Tagline:** SafeAgent enables controlled, auditable AI skill execution with enterprise-grade security at AI-native speed.

## Problem
- AI agents are difficult to govern in production.
- Existing controls are fragmented: policy, secrets, approvals, and observability live in silos.

## Solution
- Single runtime platform for agent execution security:
  - sandboxed execution
  - policy + approval orchestration
  - signed skill supply chain
  - tenant-aware rate control
  - reproducible verification gates

## Why Now
- Demand for deterministic AI controls is increasing as teams move from PoC to production.
- Procurement needs evidence, not claims: SafeAgent ships proofs in versioned artifacts.

## Editions
- **Community:** local and single-node for pilots
- **Pro:** distributed and policy-rich for production teams
- **Enterprise:** compliance, Vault/KMS, and SLA

## Security Evidence (short)
- Prompt/skill policy gates
- Network egress allowlist
- Marketplace signing + scanning
- Adversarial lab and replay checks in CI
- Verification suite (`verify`, `verify-v2`) as release prerequisite

## Commercial Position
- Predictable monthly subscription with transparent overage
- 2–4 week pilot path to production decision
- Enterprise procurement pack included in enterprise package
