# Competitive Landscape

## OpenAI / Cloud Providers

### Strength
- Mature model hosting and platform reach

### Gap
- Agent execution governance is often platform-specific and opaque
- Procurement evidence and signed skill lineage often harder to extract

## OpenClaw

### Strength
- Useful baseline safety tooling

### Gap
- Less explicit enterprise packaging and policy-to-SLA flow
- Fewer built-in commercial sales artifacts (pilot + procurement pack)

## SafeAgent

### Strength
- End-to-end control-plane + worker execution model
- Built-in adversarial verification stack and deterministic gates
- Signed package/publisher + egress policy + tenant controls
- Explicit packaging for Community/Pro/Enterprise sale motion

### Why now
- Immediate sales readiness: pricing + pilot + security/compliance documentation ready
- Evidence-driven positioning for due diligence and procurement
